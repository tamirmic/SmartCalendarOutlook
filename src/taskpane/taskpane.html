Smart Calendar
Create a calendar draft from an email
What it does
Click Smart Calendar in the ribbon while viewing an email. Smart Calendar opens a new event draft using the email subject and a short snippet of the email body.
Basic mode (current)
  • Title = email subject
  • Body = first part of email
  • Time = tomorrow at 10:00–10:30 (placeholder)
AI prefill (next)
Smart Calendar will scan the email for natural language (e.g., “next Friday afternoon”) and suggest the most likely date/time with confidence and alternatives.
